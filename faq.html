FAQ

Do I need an appointment?

Yes, appointments are recommended to ensure availability. Please contact us or book in advance to reserve your spot.

Can I wait while my vehicle is being worked on?

Our shop is a private workspace and we do not have a customer waiting area. For safety and liability reasons, we recommend dropping off your vehicle and returning once the service is complete. We will notify you as soon as your vehicle is ready for pickup.

What is your payment policy for vehicle pickup?

All invoices must be paid in full prior to vehicle pickup. Vehicles will only be released once payment has been received and confirmed.

What types of payment are accepted?

Cash, Visa, Mastercard and Interac are accepted. Gratuities are not included in the service price and are always appreciated.

Is a deposit required when booking an appointment?

Yes, a deposit is required upon invoice or drop-off. The remainder of payment is due once the job is completed.

Do I need to remove personal items from my vehicle?

Yes. Please remove all personal belongings before your appointment so we can properly clean all areas of the vehicle.

Do you need access to water or electricity for mobile detailing?

Yes, access to water and electricity may be required depending on the service.

Do you clean pet hair or heavily soiled vehicles?

Yes, but additional charges may apply depending on the level of cleaning required.

Your vehicle's appearance is our driving force.
